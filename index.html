Research project on Hemigrapsus oregonensis
Conducted in 2017
Hemigrapsus oregonens is a small shore crab. This study was done to understand the effect of temperature on the velocity of Hemigrapsus oregonensis. We have found that as temperature decreases, so will the velocity of the shore crab.
